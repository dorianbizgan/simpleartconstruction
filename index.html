Welcome !

We care about quality, and you'll know it.

Simple Art Construction is a local, family owned construction company proudly serving the greater Seattle area. We are a licensed, bonded and insured general contractor.

We specialize in remodeling kitchens and baths, we are here to make your vision a reality as well as take the hassle out of your hands. We offer services to help with interior design, planning, and project management.

Simple Art Construction has over 20 years of construction experience so we know how to get the job done right, limiting the amount of time wasted so you can start enjoying your home sooner!
